About Skills-Tracker

Established in 1992, Skills-Tracker pioneered the concept of detailed work experience tracking for apprentices and trainees. Our comprehensive suite of tools provides a centralized online platform for recording on-the-job experience with ease and precision.

We bridge the gap between trainees, training organizations, and employers to deliver the best training outcomes. Skills-Tracker consolidates weekly work entries and offers insightful reporting to assess progress. Our flexible solution adapts to your unique requirements.

How Does It Work?

Skills-Tracker streamlines documentation and verification, seamlessly linking theoretical training with practical job skills. Supervisors can validate entries, while training organizations generate detailed reports to monitor progress effectively.

What Are the Benefits?

  • Versatile: Usable for any trade, traineeship, or qualification
  • Customizable: Tailored to your specific needs
  • Verified: Permanent record for OH&S and compliance tracking
  • Bridging: Connects theory with on-the-job experience
  • Efficient: Boosts training delivery efficiency
  • Strategic: Serves as a talent development management tool
  • Focused: Ensures employees gain the right experience
  • Insightful: Reporting highlights focus areas and shop floor targets

Who Can Use Skills-Tracker?

  • Trainees
  • Apprentices
  • Post Trade Professionals
  • Undergraduates & Graduates
  • Supervisors & Managers
  • Small, Medium, and Large Companies
  • Training Organizations
